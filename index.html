🎮 What is This Game?

YuYin · Offline Fishing is a fully offline fishing collection game. No internet required — no Wi-Fi, no cellular data, no permissions needed. Open the app and start fishing immediately.

Cast your line across unique habitats, feel the bite with haptic feedback, and reel in your catch with motion controls. Collect 96 species of fish, unlock titles, climb the leaderboard, and enjoy pure, distraction-free fishing.

📱

Motion Controls

Cast your rod like the real thing! Swing your device using the built-in gyroscope and feel every vibration.

🐟

96 Fish Species

Spread across 5 unique habitats — from the Amazon rainforest to deep-sea volcanic vents. Each fish is beautifully illustrated.

🏆

Title System

Progress from Novice to Fishing God. Every catch and every achievement counts toward your growth.

🛒

Shop & Leaderboard

Buy bait, upgrade gear, and track your progress on the personal leaderboard.
